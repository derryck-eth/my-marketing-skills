# Salesforce

## What It Does
Salesforce is an enterprise CRM platform managing customer relationships, sales pipelines, lead management, and business processes at scale.

## Key Capabilities for Marketing
- **Lead management** - Capture, track, and qualify leads
- **Sales pipeline** - Visualize deals and forecast revenue
- **Account management** - Maintain relationships with key customers
- **Opportunity tracking** - Monitor sales cycles and deal progress
- **Custom fields** - Track any business-specific data
- **Reporting** - Build dashboards and reports for insights

## How to Connect via MCP
1. Log into Salesforce organization
2. Go to Setup > Apps > App Manager
3. Create or select a connected app
4. Generate OAuth credentials
5. Obtain instance URL (login.salesforce.com or custom)
6. Configure MCP with credentials

## Example Agent Queries
- "Show me all opportunities in negotiation stage worth over $100k"
- "Create an account for [company name] and add primary contact"
- "What's the sales forecast by region?"
- "Update opportunity stage based on recent activity"
- "Generate a report of closed deals from last quarter"
