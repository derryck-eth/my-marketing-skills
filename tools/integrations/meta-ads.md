# Meta/Facebook Ads

## What It Does
Meta Ads (Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network, Messenger) allows creation and management of campaigns with advanced audience targeting, pixel tracking, and conversion measurement.

## Key Capabilities for Marketing
- **Multi-platform ads** - Run ads on Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network, and Messenger
- **Advanced targeting** - Target by interests, behaviors, demographics, and custom audiences
- **Pixel tracking** - Monitor conversions and user behavior on your website
- **Dynamic ads** - Auto-serve personalized product recommendations
- **Lookalike audiences** - Find new users similar to your best customers
- **Campaign optimization** - Automatic bid and budget allocation

## How to Connect via MCP
1. Go to Meta Business Suite
2. Navigate to Settings > Apps and Websites
3. Connect your app or website
4. Create system user account for API access
5. Generate access token with necessary scopes
6. Configure MCP with token and Ad Account ID

## Example Agent Queries
- "Create a carousel ad campaign targeting ages 25-45 interested in fitness"
- "What's the ROAS for our product retargeting campaign?"
- "Build a lookalike audience of 1% similarity to top customers"
- "Sync our email list as a custom audience"
- "Run an A/B test on ad creative variations"
