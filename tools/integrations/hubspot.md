# HubSpot

## What It Does
HubSpot is an all-in-one CRM platform combining sales, marketing, and customer service tools. It manages contacts, pipelines, email campaigns, and customer interactions.

## Key Capabilities for Marketing
- **Contact management** - Centralized database of customer information
- **Sales pipeline** - Track deals through stages and forecast revenue
- **Email campaigns** - Create, send, and track marketing emails
- **Lead scoring** - Automatically prioritize high-value prospects
- **Workflows** - Automate tasks and communications
- **Reports** - Analytics on pipeline, revenue, and campaign performance

## How to Connect via MCP
1. Log into HubSpot account
2. Go to Settings > Integrations > API Key
3. Create private app or use OAuth
4. Generate access token with necessary scopes
5. Configure MCP with credentials
6. Grant access to CRM objects

## Example Agent Queries
- "Show me all deals worth over $50k in active stage"
- "Create a contact list of companies in the tech industry"
- "Send an email campaign to leads with score over 80"
- "What's our sales pipeline forecast for next quarter?"
- "Update contact properties with data from our analytics tool"
